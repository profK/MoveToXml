=====Sharing=====
**When you share something personal and private with another character, roll communication.**

|Result|Effect|
|Critical Failure|It goes sideways. The actor takes another 2 stress.|
|Failure|No result. mark an exp on Skills|
|Partial Success|The actor gets to make a stress recovery roll. But the target gets to chose one of the following:\\ <WRAP>
  - They take 1 bond with the actor
  - The actor is at -1d6 to their next attempt to manipulate the target 
  - The target is a +1d6 for their next action where the current actor is their target
</WRAP> |
|Full Success|The actor gets to make a stress recovery roll.|
|Critical Success|Both target and actor get to make stress recovery rolls.|
