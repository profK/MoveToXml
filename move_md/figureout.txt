
=====Figure Something Out=====
**When you try to figure something out you roll** ***brains*****.**

|Result|Effect|
|Critical Failure|The actor is completely flummoxed. The actor takes -1D6 to any further attempts to figure this out.|
|Failure|The actor learns nothing. Mark an exp on Talents|
|Partial Success|The actor may ask 3 yes or no questions about the situation.|
|Full Success|The actor may ask any questions they like about the situation|
|Critical Success|In addition to a full success, the actor takes +1d6 forward to your next action that uses the information discovered.|
