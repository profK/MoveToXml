
=====Harm=====
**When you try to physically harm someone, you roll** ***athletics*****.**

|Result|Effect|
|Critical Failure|Impressive counter attack! Actor takes a severe harm.|
|Failure|Miss and reposte. Actor takes a medium harm. Mark an exp on Talents|
|Partial Success|Target takes a medium harm. Target may also chose one of the following<br>:\\ <WRAP>
  * Their guard is up, -1d6 to the next Harm or Avoid Harm roll against this target. 
  * Weak counter, actor takes a light harm 
</WRAP> |
|Full Success|Target takes a medium harm.|
|Critical Success|Target takes a severe harm.|
