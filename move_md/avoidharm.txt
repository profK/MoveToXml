
=====Avoid Harm===== 


**When you try to avoid physical harm, you roll athletics** 
|Result|Effect|
|Critical Failure|Actor takes a severe harm.|
|Failure|Actor takes a medium harm. Mark an exp on Talents|
|Partial Success|Actor takes a light harm. Target may also choose one of the following:\\ <WRAP>
  * Reposition. Actor takes -1D6 to their next Harm or Avoid Harm roll 
  * Weak counter, target takes a light harm
</WRAP> |
|Full Success|Actor takes no harm|
|Critical Success|Defense and reposte.  Actor takes no harm. target takes a medium harm.|
